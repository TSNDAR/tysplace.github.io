Burning Snow

Burning Snow is a awesome little short game created by justcore available for free here on itch.io. This game was a fun little experience that reminds me of what i have heard about a short hike. The game was really simple but was inspiring and the stylized graphics were really cool and the game looked really pretty. The end of the game was very unexpected which makes this game stand out in my opinion. I highly recomend anybody with some free time to download this game and play it.
